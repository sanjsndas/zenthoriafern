Your privacy matters

We treat your data like a rare fern — with care, respect, and transparency. Below is our commitment to protecting your information.

🌱 Information we collect

Zenthoria Fern World collects only the information you voluntarily provide when you interact with our site, such as when you:

  • Subscribe to our newsletter or fern care guides
  • Fill out a contact form or request a consultation
  • Comment on our blog or participate in community discussions
  • Make a purchase or register for an event

This may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other details you choose to share. We never collect sensitive data without explicit consent.

🍃 How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide fern care support
  • To send you relevant updates, seasonal fern tips, and event invitations (you may opt out at any time)
  • To improve our website, content, and user experience
  • To process orders or registrations securely

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your trust is the root of our relationship.

🔒 Data security — We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. All information is stored on secure servers, and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

📬 Cookies & tracking

We use minimal cookies to enhance your browsing experience — such as remembering your preferences and analyzing site traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. For more details, please see our Cookies Policy.

📝 Your rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: If any information is inaccurate, you can ask us to correct it.
  • Erasure: You may request that we delete your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-out: You can unsubscribe from communications at any time by clicking the link in our emails or contacting us directly.

To exercise any of these rights, simply reach out via our contact page — we’ll respond within 24 hours.

🌿 Updates to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We’ll always post the latest version here, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
