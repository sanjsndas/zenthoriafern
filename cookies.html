Our cookies & your choice

We use cookies to nurture your browsing experience — just enough to keep things fresh, never overbearing. Learn how we use them and how you can manage your preferences.

🍪 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and improve your overall experience. At Zenthoria Fern World, we use cookies minimally and transparently.

🌿 How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies: These are necessary for the site to function — for example, to keep you logged in or remember your cookie consent.
  • Preference cookies: We use a small number of cookies to remember your settings, such as language or region, so you don’t have to set them every time.
  • Analytics cookies: We use lightweight analytics (like page views) to understand which fern articles are most popular, helping us create better content. All data is anonymised.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies from third parties. No retargeting, no behavioral ads — just a quiet, respectful digital garden.

🌱 Your choice matters — You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Blocking cookies may affect some functionality, but our core content will always remain accessible.

📌 Third-party cookies

We do not integrate with third-party ad networks or social media tracking. The only external services we use are for secure payment processing (if applicable) and occasional embedded educational videos — these may set their own cookies, but we have no control over them. We recommend reviewing their policies separately.

🔄 Updates to this policy
